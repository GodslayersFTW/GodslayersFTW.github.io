Our International Project in Skövde

One week of learning, culture, teamwork, and experiences in Sweden 🇸🇪

Saturday — Arrival Day

Today we woke up at 3:00 AM to catch our flight to Sweden. After a long journey, we finally arrived in Göteborg (where we had to wait for 3 hours because they forgot to load our luggage on the plane) and then took a train to Skövde. At approximately 5:30 PM, we arrived at our accommodation and went to the ICA Maxi for grocery shopping. We ended up being in this store for about 2 hours, which was quite an adventure! After that, we attempted to make dinner, but it turned out to be a bit of a disaster. We still ate it, but it was definitely not our best culinary creation. We had to improvise with the ingredients we had, and it was definitely an experience we won't forget! After dinner we had a few drinks and went to bed early to rest for the exciting week ahead.

Most memorable moment: Our first meal in Skövde
